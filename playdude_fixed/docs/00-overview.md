# PlayDude Overview

## Product Summary

PlayDude is a Nightlife Intelligence website with the slogan **Know Before You Go**.

The site helps readers understand nightlife cities, areas, and venues before they visit. The core user problem is uncertainty: people want to know what a place is actually like, what to expect, how to avoid awkward surprises, and whether a venue matches their preferences.

PlayDude is not a broad travel blog, party gossip site, or adult website. It is an editorial product focused on useful pre-visit intelligence.

## Initial Cities

- Bangkok
- Pattaya
- Tokyo
- Osaka

## MVP Strategy

The MVP should prove that readers want practical nightlife guidance before building complex features or paid products.

Primary MVP focus:

- City guides
- Area explainers
- Venue profiles
- Weekly editorial publishing
- Search-friendly evergreen content

Out of scope for MVP:

- Paid membership
- User accounts
- User reviews
- Booking
- Event ticketing
- Real-time venue capacity
- Community forums

## Editorial Style

The content style should feel like:

- 70% Lonely Planet: informed, composed, contextual, useful
- 30% Tripadvisor: practical, expectation-setting, experience-oriented

The voice should be direct, observant, and non-sensational.

## Business Direction

The MVP is editorial-first. Later monetization can include:

- Membership
- Advertising
- Paid city guides
- Paid venue guides
- Sponsored placements with clear labeling

## Success Definition

The initial project succeeds if readers use PlayDude to decide:

- Which nightlife area to visit
- Which venues fit their mood and budget
- What to expect before arriving
- What to avoid
- How to feel more prepared and less uncertain
