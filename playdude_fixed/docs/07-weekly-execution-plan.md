# Weekly Execution Plan

## MVP Operating Principle

Publish useful nightlife intelligence consistently before adding complex product features. The founder-editor should focus on quality, clarity, and repeatable output.

## Weekly Cadence

Recommended weekly target:

- 1 substantial guide, or
- 3 venue profiles, or
- 1 area guide plus 1 venue profile

Quality matters more than volume. A small set of strong guides is better than a broad set of shallow pages.

## Week 1: Foundation

- Finalize positioning and editorial guardrails.
- Choose the first flagship city.
- Create working content folders for each city.
- Draft the first city guide outline.
- Define venue profile fields.

Deliverable:

- One detailed city guide outline ready for drafting.

## Week 2: First Flagship Guide

- Draft the first city nightlife guide.
- Add area breakdowns.
- Add first-night plan.
- Add budget, etiquette, safety, and FAQ sections.
- Edit for PlayDude tone.

Deliverable:

- One publishable flagship city guide.

## Week 3: Supporting Area Content

- Draft area guides for the flagship city.
- Prioritize the areas most likely to answer first-time visitor questions.
- Link area guides back to the flagship city guide.

Deliverable:

- Two to four area sections or standalone area guides.

## Week 4: First Venue Batch

- Select 5-10 venues in the flagship city.
- Draft concise venue profiles.
- Add last verified dates and confidence levels.
- Link venue profiles to city and area content.

Deliverable:

- First venue profile batch.

## Week 5: Second City Guide

- Repeat the flagship guide process for the next priority city.
- Compare structure against the first guide for consistency.
- Note city-specific differences rather than forcing the same advice.

Deliverable:

- One publishable second city guide.

## Week 6: Content Review and SEO Pass

- Review all published pages for clarity.
- Improve titles and meta descriptions.
- Add internal links.
- Identify thin sections.
- Create a list of content refresh tasks.

Deliverable:

- Cleaner content library with stronger search readiness.

## Ongoing Monthly Cycle

Each month:

- Publish one new city or area guide.
- Publish one venue batch.
- Refresh older content.
- Review search data.
- Update the editorial backlog.

## Founder-Editor Checklist

Before publishing each piece:

- Does it reduce uncertainty?
- Is the reader better prepared after reading it?
- Is the tone useful rather than sensational?
- Are claims appropriately qualified?
- Are freshness signals included?
- Are related pages linked?

## Backlog Prioritization

Prioritize content that:

- Helps first-time visitors make decisions
- Has clear search demand
- Can link to multiple future pages
- Establishes PlayDude as Nightlife Intelligence
- Can later support paid guides or membership
