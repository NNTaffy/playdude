# Venue Profile Template

Use this template for PlayDude venue profiles.

## Page Title

`[Venue Name], [City]: Know Before You Go`

## Short Summary

One concise paragraph explaining what the venue is, what it feels like, and who it is best for.

## Quick Verdict

- Best for:
- Not ideal for:
- Venue type:
- Area:
- Price signal:
- Best time to arrive:
- Visitor confidence level:
- Last verified:

## What To Expect

Describe the practical experience:

- Atmosphere
- Layout
- Music or entertainment style
- Noise level
- Seating or standing room
- Pace of the night

## Crowd

Explain the likely crowd without stereotyping.

Consider:

- Local versus international mix
- Age range signal
- Solo visitor friendliness
- Group friendliness
- Date suitability

## Location Context

Explain where the venue sits in the city:

- Nearby nightlife areas
- Transport access
- Ease of finding it
- Whether it works as a first stop, main stop, or late stop

## Pricing Signals

Use ranges or signals rather than fragile exact claims.

Examples:

- Budget-friendly
- Mid-range
- Premium
- Cover charge possible
- Minimum spend possible
- Prices vary by night or event

## Entry Expectations

Cover practical uncertainty reducers:

- Dress code
- ID checks
- Reservation needs
- Queue expectations
- Cash or card notes
- Event-night differences

## Best For

List specific use cases:

- First drink in the area
- Group night out
- Low-pressure introduction to the district
- Music-focused night
- Premium experience

## Not Ideal For

Be honest about mismatch scenarios:

- Quiet conversation
- Tight budgets
- Visitors who dislike crowds
- People arriving without a reservation on peak nights

## Practical Tips

Include 3-7 concrete tips:

- When to arrive
- Where to sit or stand
- What to check before going
- How to leave safely
- Nearby backup options
