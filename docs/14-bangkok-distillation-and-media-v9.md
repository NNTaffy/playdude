# PlayDude v9 Bangkok Distillation and Media Layer

## Scope

This release distils two additional Bangkok sources into the existing English Nightlife Intelligence structure:

- Bao Bob, “Bangkok 2025 High-End Nightlife,” published September 2025: https://twbob.tw/bangkok-high-end-nightlife/
- NightAsia, “2026 Bangkok Nightlife: Eight Formats,” checked July 2026: https://nightasia.com/dark-bangkok-info/

The source pages are observations, not live price feeds. Promotional claims, booking offers, explicit service descriptions, referral contacts, and guaranteed outcomes are not reproduced.

## Content changes

- Maria Massage, Black Caviar, and The Pimp were already present. Their records now preserve the new source as a dated comparison instead of creating duplicates.
- Sherbet Club Bangkok and Czech Club Bangkok already existed in the source ledger. The new editor records are merged into those profiles, giving each one a clearer canonical name, a preserved second source, and a stronger price ledger without creating duplicate pages.
- The Bangkok First-Night Playbook now explains the eight broad formats, district selection, route logic, and conflicting price observations.
- Ratchada Soapy-Massage Intelligence now explains why a live selection range may differ from a venue's complete tier ladder.
- Bangkok Hosted-Club Budgeting now compares The Pimp, Sherbet, and Czech Club while keeping hosted time, minimum spend, bottles, rooms, and consent separate.

## Media policy

Every one of the 214 Venue Intel profiles and 26 playbooks has a unique local visual.

Selected official venue images or logos are stored locally and visibly credited to the venue. All other entries use a clearly labelled “PlayDude editorial visual.” Editorial visuals do not claim to show the building, interior, staff, or current line-up.

Google Maps user-uploaded photography is not copied. Map links remain available for navigation, but third-party photos are not republished without a clear licence or venue-controlled source.
